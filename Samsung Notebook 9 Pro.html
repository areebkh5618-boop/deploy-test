Detailed Description

The Samsung Notebook 9 Pro is designed for users who seek a blend of style, portability, and powerful performance. Powered by the Intel Core i7-8565U processor and 16GB of DDR4 RAM, this convertible laptop can handle demanding tasks with ease.
